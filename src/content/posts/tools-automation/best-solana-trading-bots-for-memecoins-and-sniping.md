## What Is a Solana Trading Bot?

A **Solana trading bot** is an automated tool that interacts directly with Solana DEXs like Jupiter, Raydium, and Orca to buy and sell tokens based on predefined rules.

Most Solana bots:
- Run inside Telegram
- Connect directly to your wallet
- Execute swaps instantly
- Allow auto-buy, auto-sell, and stop-loss logic

Because they interact directly with your wallet, **security matters more here** than with exchange bots.

Before using any bot, make sure you have:
- A secure Solana wallet
- A small funded trading wallet (not your main holdings)
