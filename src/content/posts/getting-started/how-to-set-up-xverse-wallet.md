It's available as a mobile app (iOS and Android) and Chrome browser extension, making it versatile for daily use.

### Prerequisites Before Setting Up Xverse

- A smartphone (iOS or Android) or computer with Chrome browser.
- A secure, private location to write down your recovery phrase.
- Never share your seed phrase or password with anyone—this is the key to avoiding common scams.

### Step-by-Step Guide to Setting Up Xverse Wallet

Follow these steps carefully. The process takes less than 5 minutes.

#### Step 1: Download Xverse from Official Sources

Always download from official links to avoid fake apps:

- **Mobile (Recommended for Beginners)**:
  - iOS: App Store – Search "Xverse: Bitcoin Crypto Wallet" or visit https://apps.apple.com/app/xverse-bitcoin-crypto-wallet/id1552272513
  - Android: Google Play Store – Search "Xverse Wallet" or visit the official site.
- **Browser Extension** (Great for Desktop DeFi and Ordinals):
  - Visit https://www.xverse.app/download and click the Chrome option, or go directly to the Chrome Web Store.

**Warning**: Only use https://www.xverse.app as your source. Fake sites can steal your funds.

#### Step 2: Install and Open the App

- Install the app or extension.
- Open Xverse. You'll see the welcome screen with two options: **Create New Wallet** or **Restore Wallet**.

#### Step 3: Create a New Wallet

- Select **Create New Wallet**.
- The app will generate a 12-word secret recovery phrase (also called seed phrase).
- **Write it down exactly in order** on paper. Do not screenshot or store digitally.
- Confirm the phrase by re-entering the words in the correct order.

This phrase is your master backup. If you lose your device, you can restore everything with it. Store it offline in a safe place—losing it means permanent loss of funds.

For more on seed phrase security, see our guide on [How to Transfer Crypto from Exchange to Wallet Safely in 2026](https://txchyon.com/blog/getting-started/how-to-transfer-crypto-from-exchange-to-wallet-safely/).

#### Step 4: Set Up Security

- Create a strong password (or PIN on mobile).
- Enable biometric login (Face ID or fingerprint) for convenience.
- On mobile, you can also enable additional app lock features.

Xverse encrypts everything on your device—no data is sent to servers.

#### Step 5: Explore Your New Wallet

- Your dashboard will show separate accounts for Bitcoin (Native Segwit and Taproot addresses) and Stacks/Ordinals.
- Tap **Receive** to view your Bitcoin address (starts with bc1q for Segwit or bc1p for Taproot).
- Use Native Segwit (bc1q) for regular BTC transfers to avoid higher fees.

Xverse automatically separates addresses: Taproot for Ordinals/Runes to prevent accidental spending of inscribed sats.

#### Step 6: Fund Your Wallet (Optional but Recommended)

To test everything:

- Buy BTC directly: Tap **Buy** and follow the on-ramp (partners like MoonPay or others based on your location).
- Or transfer from an exchange: Use your Xverse receive address.

### Importing an Existing Wallet into Xverse

If you already have a Bitcoin or Stacks wallet (e.g., from Unisat, Leather, Phantom, OKX, or Magic Eden):

- Choose **Restore Wallet** on the welcome screen.
- Select your previous wallet provider (Xverse makes this easy with guided import).
- Enter your 12/24-word seed phrase.
- Xverse auto-detects the correct paths and imports balances, Ordinals, and assets.

### Common Issues and Troubleshooting

- **Invalid Seed Phrase**: Double-check spelling and order.
- **No Balance Showing**: Wait for sync or refresh—Ordinals can take time to index.
- **High Fees**: Use Native Segwit addresses for regular transfers.
- Always update to the latest version for security fixes.

If issues persist, visit the official support at https://support.xverse.app.
