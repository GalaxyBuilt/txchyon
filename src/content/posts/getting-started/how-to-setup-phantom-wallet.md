### Step 2: Create Your New Wallet

1. Click "Add to Chrome" (or your browser's equivalent).
2. Pin the Phantom extension to your toolbar for easy access.
3. Click the Phantom icon that appears in your browser's toolbar.
4. Select **"Create New Wallet."**

### Step 3: The MOST IMPORTANT STEP: Your Secret Recovery Phrase

‚ö†Ô∏è **READ THIS CAREFULLY BEFORE CONTINUING:**

Your **Secret Recovery Phrase** (also called a seed phrase) is the **only way** to recover your wallet. If you lose it, you lose access to your funds forever. No one can help you recover it‚Äînot Phantom support, not any company.

**What you'll see:**
* 12 random words in a specific order.
* *Example: crystal robot blanket tomato gym abandon clever focus reason velvet success*

**Security Protocol:**

1. **Write it down on paper** (not digitally‚Äîno photos, no screenshots, no text files).
2. Store multiple copies in different, secure physical locations (e.g., safe, lockbox).
3. **Never share it with anyone.** Phantom will NEVER ask for it.
4. Consider a **metal backup plate** (like CryptoSteel) for fire/water protection.

**Complete the verification:**
* Click **"I've saved it somewhere safe."**
* Enter the words in the correct order when prompted.
* Create a **strong, unique password** for the wallet itself (this is different from your recovery phrase!).

‚úÖ **Congratulations!** Your wallet is created. You'll see your dashboard with a $0 balance.

## Part 3: Funding Your Wallet

### Option A: Receive from an Exchange (Most Common & Cost-Effective)

1. **Copy your Phantom address:** Make sure you're on the correct network tab (Solana for SOL, Ethereum for ETH) and copy your address.
2. **Go to your exchange** (Coinbase, Binance, Kraken, etc.):
    * Navigate to **"Withdraw"** or **"Send."**
    * Paste your Phantom address.
    * **CRITICAL:** Select the correct network:
        * For **SOL:** Choose **"Solana (SOL)"** network.
        * For **ETH/USDC:** Choose **"Ethereum (ERC-20)"** network.
    * Enter the amount and confirm (always do a small test first).
3. **Wait for confirmation:**
    * Solana: ~5-10 seconds.
    * Ethereum: ~1-5 minutes (can vary with congestion).

### Option B: Buy Directly in Phantom (US Only, Higher Fees)

Phantom partners with MoonPay and Coinbase Pay for direct purchases:
1. Click the **"Buy"** button in the main interface.
2. Select your preferred provider.
3. Enter the amount and complete payment details and any KYC (Know Your Customer) steps.
4. Funds typically arrive within 1-30 minutes.

*Note: Direct purchase fees are generally higher than buying on an exchange first.*

### Option C: Receive from Another Wallet

Simply share your **public address** (the one you copied earlier) with the sender. No additional steps are needed on your end.

## Part 4: Essential Operations

### How to Send Cryptocurrency

1. Click the **"Send"** button in the bottom navigation.
2. **Paste the recipient's address** (double-check for accuracy!).
3. Select the token (SOL, ETH, USDC, etc.).
4. Enter the amount.
5. Preview the transaction details (amount, network fee, total).
6. Click **"Send"** and confirm.

**Pro Tip:** Always send a small test transaction first when sending to a new address for the first time.

### How to Swap Tokens (Using the Built-in DEX)

Swap tokens without leaving your wallet:
1. Click the **"Swap"** tab.
2. Select tokens: **"You pay"** (what you're swapping from) and **"You receive"** (what you're swapping to).
3. Enter the amount.
4. Review the quote:
    * Exchange rate
    * Price impact (lower is better for large swaps)
    * Network fee
5. Click **"Swap"** and confirm.

*Supports swaps on both Solana and Ethereum networks.*

## Part 6: Critical Security Guide

### Security Best Practices

1. **Recovery Phrase Protection:**
    * Paper copies stored in fireproof/waterproof containers.
    * **Never stored digitally.**
    * Consider steel backup plates.
2. **Wallet & Device Security:**
    * Use a unique, strong password for your wallet.
    * Keep your computer and phone free of malware.
    * Use a password manager.
3. **Transaction Safety:**
    * Always verify recipient addresses (especially the first and last few characters).
    * Use the **"Copy Address"** function instead of typing.
    * Test with small amounts first.
4. **dApp & Connection Hygiene:**
    * Regularly review and revoke unused dApp connections (Settings ‚Üí Trusted Apps).
    * Only connect to reputable dApps. Double-check URLs.

### Common Scams & How to Avoid Them

* ‚ùå **Fake Support:** No legitimate support will **ever** ask for your recovery phrase or private keys.
* ‚ùå **Fake Airdrops:** If it seems too good to be true, it is. Don't interact with unexpected tokens/NFTs.
* ‚ùå **Malicious dApps:** Check URLs carefully. Look for community verification and official links.
* ‚ùå **Address Poisoning:** Scammers send $0 transactions from addresses similar to yours, hoping you'll copy theirs by mistake. Always verify the full address.

**Golden Rule:** When in doubt, pause and verify. It's better to miss an opportunity than to lose your funds permanently.

## Part 7: Troubleshooting

### "Transaction Failed" Errors

* **Insufficient funds for gas:** Ensure you have enough SOL/ETH in your wallet to cover network fees.
* **Slippage too low:** When swapping volatile tokens, increase your slippage tolerance in the swap settings (start with 1%).
* **Network congestion:** (Mostly for Ethereum). Wait and try again later, or adjust your gas fee if the wallet allows.

### "Cannot Read Balance" or Sync Issues

1. Check your internet connection.
2. Switch networks (Solana ‚Üî Ethereum) and switch back.
3. Clear the wallet cache: Settings ‚Üí **"Privacy & Security"** ‚Üí **"Clear Cache."**
4. As a last resort, reinstall the extension (you can restore with your recovery phrase).

### How to Restore Your Wallet (Lost Access)

1. On a new install, click **"I already have a wallet."**
2. Enter your **12-word recovery phrase** in the exact order.
3. Set a new password.
4. **Warning:** Only perform restoration on a trusted, malware-free device.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q: Is Phantom Wallet free?**
**A:** Yes, Phantom is completely free to download and use. You only pay blockchain network fees (gas) for transactions.

**Q: Can I use Phantom on multiple devices?**
**A:** Yes! Install Phantom on another device and select **"I already have a wallet."** Use your recovery phrase to restore. Your wallet will sync across devices.

**Q: What happens if the Phantom company disappears?**
**A:** Your funds are safe on the blockchain. You can use your secret recovery phrase to restore your wallet in any other compatible wallet (e.g., Solflare, Backpack for Solana; MetaMask for Ethereum).

**Q: Can I stake SOL directly in Phantom?**
**A:** Yes! Click on your SOL balance ‚Üí **"Stake"** ‚Üí choose a validator. Consider supporting smaller, non-top-10 validators to help decentralize the network.

**Q: How do I update Phantom?**
**A:** Browser extensions usually update automatically. For manual checks, go to `chrome://extensions/`, enable **"Developer mode,"** and click **"Update."**

**Q: Is Phantom better than MetaMask?**
**A:** For Solana, Phantom is the dominant, feature-rich choice. For Ethereum, it offers a cleaner, more user-friendly interface comparable to MetaMask. Many users run both wallets.
