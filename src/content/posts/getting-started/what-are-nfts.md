### What Does "Non-Fungible" Mean?

To understand NFTs, start with the opposite: **fungible** assets.

- Fungible: Interchangeable and identical (e.g., $1 bills, Bitcoin—1 BTC is always equal to another BTC).
- Non-Fungible: Unique and not interchangeable (e.g., a concert ticket, a trading card, or original artwork).

An NFT is a digital certificate of ownership for a specific item, recorded immutably on the blockchain.

Most NFTs follow standards like ERC-721 (Ethereum) or similar on other chains, containing metadata (description, image link, traits) that makes each one distinct.

### How Do NFTs Work?

1. **Creation (Minting)**: An artist or creator uploads a file (image, video, music) and mints it as an NFT on a blockchain.
2. **Storage**: The actual file is usually stored off-chain (e.g., IPFS or centralized servers), while the NFT token points to it via a URL in the metadata.
3. **Ownership**: The blockchain records who owns the token—transferable like any crypto asset.
4. **Verification**: Anyone can verify authenticity and provenance through the public ledger.

This solves the "right-click save" problem by providing provable scarcity and ownership history.

### Popular NFT Examples in 2026

Some iconic collections that shaped the space:

- **CryptoPunks**: 10,000 pixel-art characters on Ethereum—one of the first NFT projects.
- **Bored Ape Yacht Club (BAYC)**: 10,000 apes that granted community access and IP rights.

### Benefits of NFTs

- **True Digital Ownership**: Provenance and scarcity in the digital world.
- **Creator Royalties**: Automatic payments on secondary sales.
- **Utility**: Access to communities, events, games, or real-world perks.
- **Interoperability**: Many projects allow using NFTs across platforms.
- **Democratization**: Artists sell directly to global audiences.

### Risks and Common Criticisms

- **Volatility**: Prices can crash—many NFTs lose value.
- **Scams and Rug Pulls**: Fake projects or abandoned collections.
- **Environmental Concerns**: Older proof-of-work minting (largely mitigated by Ethereum's switch and efficient platforms).
- **Link Rot**: If off-chain storage fails, the NFT may point to nothing.
- **Hype-Driven**: Avoid FOMO—see [Crypto Investing Psychology: Mastering Emotions and Avoiding FOMO as a Beginner in 2026](/crypto-investing-psychology-beginners-2026).

Always research thoroughly and start small.
