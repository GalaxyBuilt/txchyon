## Introduction: Why MetaMask for Beginners

MetaMask is the leading non-custodial wallet for Ethereum and EVM chains, with millions of users. It connects to DeFi, NFTs, and dApps seamlessly.

In 2026, it supports multiple chains, improved security, and mobile/browser versions.

This tutorial covers full setup, seed backup, networks, and tips.

Link to our [best wallets overview](/blog/getting-started/best-crypto-wallets-for-beginners-in-2026) and [transfer guide](/blog/getting-started/how-to-transfer-crypto-from-exchange-to-wallet-safely-in-2026).

## Step 1: Install MetaMask

- Browser: Chrome/Firefox/Brave/Edge store → search "MetaMask" → official (millions downloads).
- Mobile: App Store/Google Play → official MetaMask app.

## Step 2: Create or Import Wallet

Open and choose "Create a Wallet".

Agree to data terms (opt out if preferred).

Set strong password.

## Step 3: Backup Seed Phrase

Reveal 12-word phrase.

Write on paper, store securely offline.

Confirm by reordering words.

Never screenshot or share — loss means permanent fund loss.

## Step 4: Complete Setup

Wallet ready! Pin extension for access.

## Adding Custom Networks (Layer-2 for Lower Fees)

For cheaper transactions:
- Settings → Networks → Add Network.
- Popular: Base, Arbitrum, Optimism, Polygon.

## Receiving and Sending ETH/Tokens

- Click address to copy.
- Send: Paste recipient, amount, adjust gas if needed.

Test small amounts first.

## Security Best Practices

- Verify URLs (metamask.io).
- Ignore unsolicited messages.
- Use hardware wallet integration for large amounts.
- Enable transaction simulations.
