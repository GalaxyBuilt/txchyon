## What Is Algorithmic Rebalancing?

Algorithmic rebalancing uses **rules and scripts** to automatically adjust portfolio allocations:

- Trigger trades when allocations drift  
- Maintain target exposure without manual intervention  
- Can integrate with APIs and portfolio trackers

## Key Benefits

- Reduces emotional bias  
- Saves time for large portfolios  
- Maintains discipline and consistency

1. Define target allocations
