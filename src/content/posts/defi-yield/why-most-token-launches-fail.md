## How the X Algorithm Works in 2026

X’s algorithm now prioritizes:

1. **Replies → Massive Distribution**  
   Your replies get pushed into other people’s feeds, giving you free visibility.

2. **Conversation Depth → Ranking Boost**  
   Threads with multiple rapid replies get superboosted.

3. **Early Engagement → Determines Your Reach**  
   Posts are tested on a small sample; if replies come quickly, X amplifies them.

4. **Follower Count → Low Priority**  
   You can have 50k followers but get 200 views if your account is inactive.

This is why the reply-guy strategy works so well in 2026.
