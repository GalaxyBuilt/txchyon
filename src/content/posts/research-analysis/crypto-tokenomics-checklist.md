## Introduction: Why a Tokenomics Checklist is Essential

Evaluating a crypto project can be complex, with multiple factors influencing success or failure. A **structured checklist** helps investors systematically assess:

- Token supply and distribution  
- Allocation fairness and vesting schedules  
- Utility and incentives  
- Governance and adoption potential  
- Red flags and concentration risks

## Step 7: Red Flags

- Unclear total supply or emission: ✅ / ❌  
- Misaligned allocations: ✅ / ❌  
- Unsustainable incentives: ✅ / ❌

- Weak utility or governance: ✅ / ❌  

**Check:** Any ❌ marks indicate elevated risk.
