## Introduction: Why a Checklist Matters

Even seasoned crypto investors can overlook critical details in fast-moving markets. A **structured checklist** ensures every project is analyzed consistently, reducing errors and increasing confidence in your investment decisions.

## Step 1: Project Context

- Investment goal: short-term trade vs long-term hold  
- Position size relative to portfolio  
- Key focus areas: team, tokenomics, on-chain metrics  

---

## Step 2: Value Proposition

- Clear problem statement  
- Necessity of blockchain  
- Competitive differentiation  
- Target users and adoption potential  

---

## Step 3: Tokenomics

- Total supply vs circulating supply  
- Emission schedule and inflation rate  
- Team, investor, and advisor allocations  
- Vesting periods and unlocks  
- Alignment with community incentives  

---

## Step 4: Team & Governance

- Founder and team experience  
- Prior project track record  
- Public presence and accountability  
- Governance structure and token holder influence  

---

## Step 5: On-Chain Metrics

- Top wallet distribution and concentration  
- Whale accumulation and distribution  
- Liquidity pool depth and movement  
- Active user count and transaction volume  

Refer to [On-Chain Due Diligence: Wallet Analysis](/blog/research-analysis/on-chain-due-diligence-wallet-analysis) for a deeper dive.

---

## Step 6: Market & Product

- Competitor landscape  
- Adoption metrics and user growth  
- Product-market fit assessment  
- Network effects or defensibility  

---

## Step 7: Red Flags

- Unrealistic ROI or yield promises  
- Anonymous or unverifiable team  
- Token concentration in few wallets  
- Sudden liquidity changes  
- Weak governance or opaque decision-making  

See [Red Flags That Kill Crypto Investments](/blog/research-analysis/crypto-investment-red-flags) for a full list.

---

## Step 8: Legal, Regulatory & Security Checks

- Audits or security reviews  
- Regulatory compliance and legal structure  
- Insurance coverage or contingency plans  

---

## Step 9: Synthesis & Decision

- List top pros and cons  
- Evaluate risk-reward ratio  
- Document findings for future reference  
- Determine whether to proceed with investment
